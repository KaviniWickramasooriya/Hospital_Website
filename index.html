About Us



Welcome to MediHelp Hospital !

Medihelp Hospitals stands as a prominent healthcare provider in Sri Lanka, recognized for its excellence. With a history spanning over four decades, we have significantly expanded our network of medical facilities. Our services now encompass inpatient and outpatient care, laboratories and sample collection centers, pharmacies, and medical imaging units.

Medihelp is committed to a patient-centric approach, emphasizing affordability, accessibility, and the provision of comprehensive facilities coupled with top-tier medical expertise. With a strategic presence in key locations, we are dedicated to consistently delivering quality and affordable healthcare to all Sri Lankans.

Services

Eyes care

Offering a range of eye care services, our team of Ophthalmology specialists/eye surgeons employs world-class diagnostic and surgical equipment, tailoring treatment to your individual needs.

Pulomonary

Pulmonary critical care medicine, is a specialized field of emergency medicine that focuses on patients suffering from injuries and diseases of the respiratory or pulmonary system, which includes the lungs, trachea, diaphragm, and related structures.

Neurology

The Neurology service diagnoses, treats and manages disorders that affect the nervous system. We are also involved in clinical research, clinical trials, as well as basic research and translational research.

Dental Care

The dental services definition refers maintaining the oral hygiene. There are many types of dental services out there, but major dental services types are related to periodontists and orthodontists.

Cardiology

Our cardiology services help people with cardiovascular disease manage their condition day-to-day. Through clinical consultations, cardiologists provide support and treatment to patients.

Hepatology

The Hepatology service specialises in the investigation and treatment of all types of liver disease, including viral hepatitis, fatty liver disease, and rarer conditions, including those patients who have received or require liver transplantation.
